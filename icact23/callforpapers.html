INSTRUCTIONS TO AUTHORS

The authors willing to present papers in the conference are requested to submit the full length paper not exceeding 6 pages (in IEEE format) by e-mail as an attachment to icact2023@gmail.com on or before the last date for submission. All the papers will be peer reviewed by the experts. The authors of selected paper will be intimated through e-mail. The first author should incorporate the reviewers' comments and submit the camera ready paper along with the registration form. The registration fee includes Conference kit, refreshments and working lunch. Accommodation will be provided based on prior request with additional cost.
